Tutorial Overview

This tutorial provides a comprehensive introduction to temporal information retrieval and question answering in the context of modern Large Language Models (LLMs). As information becomes increasingly time-sensitive, understanding temporal dynamics in retrieval and QA systems is crucial for building effective AI applications.

Key Topics Covered

  • Fundamentals of temporal information retrieval
  • Temporal question answering architectures
  • Integrating temporal reasoning with LLMs
  • Recent advances and benchmarks
  • Real-world applications and case studies
  • Challenges and future research directions

Target Audience

This tutorial is designed for researchers, practitioners, and students interested in information retrieval, natural language processing, and large language models. Participants should have basic knowledge of NLP and machine learning concepts.

Learning Outcomes

After attending this tutorial, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the unique challenges of temporal information retrieval
  • Design and implement temporal QA systems
  • Leverage LLMs for temporal reasoning tasks
  • Evaluate temporal IR and QA systems using standard benchmarks
  • Identify promising research directions in this area
